Second Life

by kitty ray

1,464 minutes | 406 streams

I’d describe this song as ethereal and romantic, with DnB influences and delicate electronic trills. It feels so light and airy that it almost makes me feel like I’m floating. I especially resonate with the lyric, “You make me this quiet thing that I’m not used to being.”

Cigarettes out the Window

by TV Girl

1,055 minutes | 351 streams

This indie pop song makes me want to dance in a soft, swaying motion, especially as the melody bounces between the left and right ear like an echo. It has a subtle, nostalgic energy that I admire.

Oh Yuh

by Kempachii

633 minutes | 335 streams

This hip-hop song with electronic elements has a solid beat that’s easy to bop along to. Unfortunately, it got in my rotation during my chronically online era. Looking back now, the lyrics are honestly so ridiculous that I’m embarrassed it ended up in my top songs.

Found Footage

by Take/Five

308 minutes | 321 streams

This liminal and inspiring album intro blends a soft music box melody with the moon landing audio and a podcast clip about failure. It has become a motivator for me when life gets tough, especially with the line, “But failure is one of the most important things you could ever have.”
